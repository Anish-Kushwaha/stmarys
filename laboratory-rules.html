Laboratory Rules

Students must wear lab coat in the laboratory for chemistry practicals.

Perfect silence in the laboratory is essential for concentration and successful scientific work.

  • Follow the teacher's instructions before handling equipment.
  • Keep chemicals and instruments in their proper places.
  • Report breakage or accidents immediately.

Rules for the School Library

Students should keep in mind that “Reading makes a full man”. They should develop the habit of reading, systematically and thoughtfully.

  • Use library books carefully and return them on time.
  • Maintain silence and reading discipline in the library.
  • Keep the library neat so every student can benefit from it.
